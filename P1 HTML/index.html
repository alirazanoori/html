DR.ISRAR AHMED

Dr. Israr Ahmed (1932-2010) was a renowned Pakistani Islamic scholar, theologian, and preacher known for his profound understanding of the Quran and Islamic teachings. He founded the Tanzeem-e-Islami organization, aiming to establish an Islamic state based on the principles laid out in the Quran and Sunnah. Dr. Israr was an eloquent speaker who captivated audiences with his articulate lectures and passionate advocacy for Islamic revivalism. He emphasized the importance of understanding the Quran in its original context and encouraged Muslims to strive for a deeper comprehension of their faith. His legacy persists through his numerous written works, lectures, and the institution he established, continuing to inspire and guide individuals in their spiritual journeys.
